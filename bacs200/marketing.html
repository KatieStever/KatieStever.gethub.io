About

We are are non-profit ogranization which alleviates hunger and proverty by providing
food and servives to people that really need it.

Last year we were able to provide over 14.6 million pounds of food.

We are lucky enough to be part of Feeding America.

How we do what we do

We are able to get our food from farmers/argicultural Donors,Producers & Distributors. We also get some from Grocery chains, retail & purchased food. And lastlly we get food from Food Drives, USDA commodities & Feeding America

After we get that food, we get to give it out. We give it out through Mobile Food PAntry, Kids Cafe, Summer Feeding, Senior Feeding, School Backpacks, School Pantries, Emergency Food Boxes, Farms to Families and Nonprofit Agency Partners
